Prototype Write Up

Interactively Visualizing the Differences Between Lungs Across Multiple Lung Conditions

Gahn Wuwong, Nathaphat Taleongpong, Jonathan Gu

What have you done so far?

We have created a prototype that visualizes the differences between lungs across multiple lung conditions. The prototype allows users to explore the data interactively and compare the mechanical properties of the respiratory system in participants with different lung conditions.

What will be the most challenging of your project to design and why?

The most challenging part of our project will be designing an intuitive and user-friendly interface that effectively communicates the differences between lungs across multiple lung conditions. We need to ensure that the visualization is clear, informative, and engaging for users with varying levels of expertise in respiratory physiology.
